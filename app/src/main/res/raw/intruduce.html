Homepage

Here you can browse the verse list or search for a word within it.
The displayed verse is then the learning verse for the exercises.





Learning Methods



Word Click

Simply put, click on the word.
Optionally, Language to Text can be set to "easy to recognize"
The up arrow has additional features.


Word Mix


Click on the word, then again on the place where it should go.
(If correct, it jumps to the correct place)

Letters


Click on the blue or orange letter fields.
Below you can see the moves, e.g., 5 | 20 5 moves by users out of at least 20.

Speaking


Here you can memorize. Requires TTS text-to-speech and speech recognition.
Verse text can also be partially learned by tapping a word (from-to). All this with many customization options.

Reciting


Similar to speaking, but via an input field where you practice reciting with the "keyboard speech recognition," which works better. Much has been optimized here as well. Requires TTS text-to-speech and speech recognition. Verse text can also be partially learned by tapping on a word (from-to). All this with many customization options.
